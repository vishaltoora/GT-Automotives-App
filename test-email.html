GT Automotives

Email Logo Issue Explanation

Why you're seeing a placeholder:

  • Gmail and many email clients block data URI (base64) images for security reasons
  • The logo is embedded correctly, but blocked by your email client
  • This is expected behavior for security

Solutions:

  1. For Production: Deploy logo.png to https://gt-automotives.com/logo.png
  2. For Testing: Use a public image hosting service (Imgur, CloudFlare, etc.)
  3. Alternative: Use CID attachments (more complex but better supported)

Recommendation: When you deploy the next build, make sure logo-email.png is copied to the public directory and accessible at https://gt-automotives.com/logo.png
